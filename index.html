Mint Discs

Web Development 201: Interaction Design

Fall 2021

Role Played: I was one of two UX designers working on the project. We both had equal say and collaborated on each decision.

Project Description: Redesign the site to be less confusing and have a better flow.

Different Deliverables: Mood Board, Personas, Low Fidelity Wireframe, High Fidelity Wireframe, Site Mapping.


Initial Mint Discs Website





Early Designs



Our mood board and low fidelity wireframe show some of the early thoughts we had for the project. Decisions such as the pink out of stock button were scrapped because it wouldn't coincide with the users previous design understandings.





Finalized Designs



Our high-fidelity pages show a much better flow to the page along with eliminating confusing elements from the original site which was a main goal of ours.



Results

Final Product: Overall, the design ended up being something that me and my partner were proud of.

Feedback: Unfortunately, since this was a project done without the knowledge of Mint Discs, we were unable to receive feedback from the company to improve our design.
